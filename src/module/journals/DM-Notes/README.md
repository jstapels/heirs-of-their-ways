## Major Story Arcs

### Arc 1: The Missing Shipments (Sessions 0-11)
- Investigate disrupted trade
- Discover bandit operation at Stonebridge Keep
- Fight Mara Stonefist and Terrak
- First encounter with Selene and the Aether Orbs
- Meet Luminara in the Fey Grotto

### Arc 2: House Arrest & Investigation (Sessions 12-14)
- Return to Loamvale under suspicion
- Investigate Lucien Darkwood connection
- Bard duel at Vine & Valor
- Witness Seraphine vs Selene confrontation

### Arc 3: Briar Hollow (Sessions 15-25)
- Travel to Briar Hollow
- Encounter Vesmir's cursed fey band
- Meet and lose Wulfric to lycanthropes
- Establish The Bottomless Goat tavern
- Navigate Baba Yaga's Hut
- Lord Varrick's visit and proposition
- Defeat Malrik Veynor at the Bone Mill

## Key NPCs Encountered

- **Luminara** - Archfey, gave blessings and Aether Orb lore
- **Cornelius** - Pombero, mischievous fey ally
- **Selene** - Seraphine's twin, works with Varrick
- **Lord Varrick** - Shadow lord, possibly undead
- **Lucien Darkwood** - Behind-the-scenes antagonist
- **Mara Stonefist** - Bandit leader (deceased)
- **Terrak** - Elemental enforcer (deceased)
- **Malrik Veynor** - Necromancer (deceased)
- **Vargr Bloodfang** - Werewolf pack alpha
- **Liora** - Wererat, Mats' lost love

## Party Accomplishments

- Cleared Stonebridge Keep
- Obtained multiple Aether Orbs
- Established The Bottomless Goat bastion
- Recovered Builder's Grace hammer
- Defeated the Marrow Titan
- Received blessings from Luminara

## Unresolved Threads

- Lord Varrick's true nature and plans
- Lucien Darkwood's location and goals
- Selene/Sylvaine's allegiance
- Liora and the werewolf pack
- The full purpose of the Aether Orbs
- Ilizar's debt to Zzyzx
- The watering hole north of the Bottomless Goat (Mishko encountered sharks/hags)

## Upcoming Adventures

### The Sunken Village of Coral Veil
*See: adventures/coral-veil/overview.md*

Beneath the massive watering hole north of the Bottomless Goat lies Coral Veil, an ancient sea elf settlement threatened by an aboleth drawn by a Water Aether Orb.

**Hook:** A sea elf messenger collapses at the Bottomless Goat, begging for help.

**Key Elements:**
- Second Aether Orb (Water aspect)
- Connects to Mishko's earlier underwater encounter
- Will draw Varrick's continued interest
- Potential alliance with sea elf village

**Estimated Sessions:** 2-3
