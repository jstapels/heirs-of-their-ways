# Session 12 - A Wolf in Sheep's Shadows

## Overview

The party debates returning to Loamvale versus pressing on to Rivermeet, ultimately choosing to head home. During their journey, they experience increasingly ominous nights before being attacked by wolves led by a terrifying Shadow Dire Wolf. Upon returning to Loamvale, they face unexpected accusations from the Council.

## Key Events

### Decision to Return Home

**At the Keep:**
- Party discusses implications of recent battles
- Debate whether they've uncovered full truth behind disrupted shipments
- Alara prepares to return to Loamvale
- Group debates following her or pressing to Rivermeet for answers
- After deliberation (and subtle DM nudge), opt to return home
- Wary of what noble families will say about success or failures

### The Journey Home

**First Night:**
- Eerie quiet throughout the night
- Distant howls of wolves
- Mishko uses magic abilities to decipher meaning
- Finds cries unsettling and unnatural

**Second Night:**
- Silence of forest grows heavier
- Foreboding weight settles over group
- Threk dons armor, sensing something amiss

### Attack of the Shadow Wolves

**Ilizar's Nightmare:**
- During Ilizar's watch, experiences vivid nightmare
- Blurs lines between dream and reality
- Wakes with start
- Finds glowing eyes staring from darkness
- Sight mirrors his dream too closely

**The Attack Begins:**
- Threk and Ilizar rouse others
- Wolves materialize from shadows
- Unnatural hunger drives them closer
- Mishko attempts to reason with them
- Wolves under sway of something sinister: their alpha

**Shadow Dire Wolf Emerges:**
- Monstrous creature woven from living shadow
- Form flickers in and out of dim torchlight
- Howl sends chill through party
- Launches assault, striking Mishko down
- Vanishes into night

**The Battle:**
- Swift and brutal combat
- Group fights off lesser wolves
- Threk, Mishko, and Ilizar attempt to pin down Shadow Wolf
- Threk's guiding bolt strikes true
- Sears creature with radiant light (weakness noted)
- Party rallies as alpha wolf calls reinforcements
- Eerie howl summons more wolves

**Critical Moments:**
- Alara caught off guard, nearly torn apart by shadowy jaws
- Ilizar intervenes, keeping her alive
- Mishko insults beast in its own tongue
- Threk's divine fury burns against darkness
- Creature slunk away into night
- Perished, form vanishing back into void

**Aftermath:**
- Forest remains deathly still - too still
- Wolves have fled
- Something about their presence lingers
- Something drove wolves east from lands west of Loamvale
- Party knows this is no coincidence
- Greater force at work
- Shadows in night growing deeper

### Return to Loamvale

**Arrival:**
- Weary but victorious
- Not met with expected celebration
- Summoned to appear before Council of Loamvale

**Council Meeting:**
- Held outside Blackthorn Estate villa
- Air thick with scent of fine wines and weight of judgment
- Ilizar chooses to skip out, not wanting to report

**Council Members Present:**
- Alejandro Escrinos
- Matriarch Tona Shadowtusk
- Bram Yran
- Josep Wolansk
- Montebank Blackthorn
- Aldric Stormwind

**The Accusation:**
- Party presents findings and proof of neutralized bandit threat
- Victory turns sour when they hand over mysterious note from 'L.D.'
- Note unknowingly implicates them in conspiracy

>
> Remember, secrecy is paramount. Do not fail me.

**Council Reactions:**
- Immediate and divided
- Alejandro Escrinos and Josep Wolansk particularly skeptical
- Question why note suggests party deliberately interfered with trade
- Montebank Blackthorn remains unreadable
- Bram Yran more intrigued than accusatory
- Aldric Stormwind takes evidence gravely
- Suggests further investigation needed before judgment

**Party's Defense:**
- Best efforts to argue innocence
- Council not fully convinced
- Note serves as damning evidence
- Family histories make them outsiders within own houses
- Party in precarious position

**Verdict:**
- Confined to house arrest
- Investigation continues into what really happened

## Notable Details

- Ilizar's nightmare preceded the attack (prophetic visions?)
- Shadow Dire Wolf is vulnerable to radiant damage
- Wolves were driven east by something even more dangerous
- Shadow creatures suggest necromantic or dark magic involvement
- The note was magically altered to implicate the party
- Party members already considered outsiders by their families
- Under house arrest pending investigation
- Ilizar avoided the Council meeting

## Cliffhanger

The party finds themselves under house arrest, accused of the very crimes they were sent to solve. How will they prove their innocence and uncover who altered the note?
