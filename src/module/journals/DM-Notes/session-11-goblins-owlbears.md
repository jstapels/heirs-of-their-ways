# Session 11 - Beaks, Blades, and Bad Decisions

## Overview

After emerging from the mystical grotto, the party battles a large goblin force led by Gribnack, including two corrupted owlbears. Following the victory, they encounter Cornelius again and receive warnings about the corrupted land before returning to the keep.

## Key Events

### Battle with Goblins and Owlbears

**Enemy Forces:**
- Large band of goblins
- Led by cunning Gribnack and elite warriors
- Two massive owlbears (clearly corrupted)
- Vines and thorns twisted around owlbears' bodies
- Unnatural corruption affecting the creatures

**Combat Highlights:**
- Fierce battle with arrows from hidden goblin archers
- Brutal melee combat in the fray
- Mats fights furiously, landing powerful blows but suffering many in return
- Mishko uses magic and misdirection to hold the line
- Ilizar attempts to speak to owlbears in Sylvan
- Tries to break their servitude
- Threk overwhelmed, taking heavy wounds trying to hold back the tide
- Through determination and well-placed strikes, party prevails

**Outcome:**
- Gribnack brought down
- One monstrous owlbear defeated
- Second owlbear, confused and unsure, flees into woods
- Gublunk (other goblin leader) escapes into woods

### Captured Goblin

**Interrogation Attempt:**
- Party captures one goblin alive
- Language barrier: goblin speaks only its native tongue
- Unable to communicate
- Group decides to end his life rather than risk complications

### Cornelius Returns - A Changed Fey

**The Meeting:**
- Pass through rocky outcroppings where they previously met Cornelius
- Cornelius waiting for them
- Seems different: more solemn, more respectful
- Acknowledges they've been touched by Luminara
- Recognizes them as true friends of the Fey

**Gift and Information:**
- Gifts Mishko a magical stone
- Stone imbued with single use of summoning spell
- Shares information about ley lines
- Warns about dangerous corruption of the land
- Someone/something is behind goblins' strange experiments
- Urges caution, particularly regarding Æther Orbs
- Orbs remained undisturbed for centuries until now

### Return to the Keep

- Continue journey with minds full of new information
- Bodies weary from battle
- Reach the keep
- Alara and Hunters have been hard at work
- Keep is cleaned and secured
- Temporary haven established

**Realization:**
- Mission far from over
- Threats in Loamvale only beginning to reveal themselves
- Much more awaits in days ahead

## NPCs Encountered

The party has a temporary haven at the keep, but the true scope of the threats facing Loamvale is only beginning to reveal itself. The Æther Orbs, corrupted creatures, and mysterious forces all point to a larger conspiracy.
