# Party Magic Items

Items acquired during the campaign by the player characters.

## Threk's Items

### Hivewarden Splint Mail
*Armor (splint), requires attunement*
Magical armor, details TBD.

### Sylvan Talon Rapier
*Weapon (rapier), requires attunement*
A fey-touched weapon.

### Shield of Expression +1
*Armor (shield), uncommon*
+1 shield that can display different expressions.

### Helm of Telepathy
*Wondrous item, requires attunement*
Found in the underwater grotto with the sea hags. Allows telepathic communication.

### Broken Promise Ring
*Ring, cursed*
From Aveline's garden. Thorns grew into Threk's finger when put on. Has charming effects.

### Wand of Entangle
*Wand*
Root-woven wand from Aveline Rosewood. Has vine/control effects.

### Aspect of Grounded Resolve
*Blessing from Luminara*
- 1/long rest: Reroll failed save vs. charm/fear from fey
- 1/long rest: Enter Grounded Resolve for 1 minute (Tremorsense 10ft, advantage vs. push/prone)

### Aspect of the Earth
*Blessing*
Proficiency bonus times per day, use reaction for +2 AC as dust and dirt swirl.

*Weapon (shortsword), requires attunement*
Found in the Fey Grotto with Altharion's remains.

**Appearance:** Silvery-green blade that glows faintly. Hilt etched with vines and flowers, wrapped in soft green leather. Pommel has shifting gemstone like moonlit pond.

**Properties:**
- Grants ability to understand Sylvan
- Can entangle targets (used against Selene)
- Additional magical properties

## Party Items (Shared)

### Aether Orb (Necrotic Aspect)
*Legendary artifact*
Recovered from the Bone Mill after defeating Malrik Veynor and the Marrow Titan. Extremely powerful and dangerous.

### Heart Container Relic
Found among the Marrow Titan's remains.

### Folding Boat
*Wondrous item*
Found in the Bone Mill. Can transform between a box, a boat, and a ship.

### Battlecaster Cards
A deck of cards found in the bandit caves, mid-game.

### Mr. Tip-Dip
*Mysterious goat companion*
A strange, unsleeping goat given by Vesmir's fey band. Has shown unusual magical nature:
- Never sleeps
- Produced a magical rope
- Coughed up a potion of greater healing
- Coughed up a potion of acid
- Seems to have a "bottomless" stomach

### The Bottomless Goat (Bastion)
The party's tavern/bastion in Briar Hollow, built on Wulfric's former farm.

**Features:**
- Sanctuary to Shyhad
- Library and arcade study
- Garden plots
- Workshop with tools
- Magical sapling from World Seed Acorn
