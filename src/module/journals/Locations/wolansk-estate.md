# Wolansk Estate

The Wolansk (/Voh Lansk/) Estate is a greater household in the Loamvale region, widely known for their many varieties of Ale. The Wolanskis are a Triton family with deep connections to the waterways of Loamvale.

## Crest
The Wolansk family crest features a light blue and purple octopus emblem.

## Facilities

### Wolansk Estate Brewery
A collection of brewing buildings and fermentation tanks on river islands and over the water at a confluence of rivers, connected by bridges.

### Wolansk Estate Brewhouse
A bottom floor bar and shop where customers drink, converse, and take tours. The living area is adjacent at the water's edge.

### Wolansk Grains and Hops
Fields of grains and barley span for miles outside Loamvale, harvested for brewing. Large lattices of hops grow along farmhouses and grain silos.

## Notable Personages

### Josep Wolansk
*Patriarch & Master Brewer*

The patriarch of the Wolansk family and master brewer. Josep is a stern, old-fashioned Triton. Seldom without stained hands and clothing, his concept of dressing formal is wearing freshly-washed denim overalls.

### Dariya Wolansk
*River Guard*

Mishko's slightly older sister. Eagerly volunteered for the family job of guarding the Willowrush River and waterways of Loamvale.

### Nikolay Bogdanic
*River Guard*

One of Mishko's cousins, son of Josep's brother Yuri. Joined the force to protect the waterways of Loamvale alongside Dariya.

## The Willowrush River

The Wolansk family has taken responsibility for protecting the Willowrush River, the main waterway through Loamvale. Family members serve as river guards, ensuring safe passage for trade shipments.

## Connection to Player Characters

Mishko Wolansk is a member of this family, representing the Wolansk Estate in the current adventure. Though his father Josep hoped the adventure would make Mishko more interested in the family brewing business, Mishko has shown more interest in music and performance.
