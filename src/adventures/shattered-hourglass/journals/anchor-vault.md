# The Anchor Vault

*Location: Lower Temple Trial 3*

## Area Description

> *A circular chamber of chained hourglasses hangs above black water. One chain is freshly snapped. Broken glass floats in slow circles, never touching the floor.*

## Interactive Features

- **Chain Winch:** A creature can use an action and [[/check Athletics 15]] to reposition one suspended hourglass, creating temporary cover or a hazard drop.
- **Old House Seals:** Wax-impressed sigils on anchor crates tie this vault to Loamvale-era sponsorship. On [[/check History 14]], identify which house funded which ritual supply line.
- **Blackwater Reflection:** Peering into the water shows recent movement trails from the rival team, granting advantage on one check to anticipate their tactics.

## Core Scene

This chamber introduces the rival faction before the finale.

### Rival Strike Team (Hard-Deadly)
- @UUID[Compendium.heirs-of-their-ways.heirs-actors.ShHrKestrelVan01]{Kestrel Vane}
- **Printed Stat Blocks (Monster Manual 2024):** 1 Veteran, 3 Scouts, 1 Mage

### Alternate Resolution

Kestrel can be bargained with:
- [[/check Persuasion 16]] + meaningful concession (loot split, access rights, or nonaggression pact)

On success, Kestrel withdraws and provides one practical warning about the Chrono-Hydra's lair timing.
