# Archive of Unfinished Futures

*Location: Lower Temple Trial 2*

## Area Description

> *Stone shelves slide and reshuffle on their own, whispering names and events that have not happened. On each tablet, scratched notes describe futures that fork and die.*

## Interactive Features

- **Forecast Tablets:** Each character can read one possible future tied to a personal fear or ambition. Treat as narrative leverage, not fixed prophecy.
- **Loamvale Trade Cipher:** A narrow shelf contains old supply routes signed with house merchant marks. On [[/check Investigation 14]], reveal one local landmark connected to current campaign travel.
- **Resonant Bell Plate:** Ringing the plate freezes shelf movement for 1 round, creating cover lanes.

### Alternate Route

The party can chart a safe route through the archive by completing:
- [[/check Arcana 16]]
- [[/check Investigation 15]]

If both checks succeed before two failures, the party bypasses guardian activation and recovers one bonus clue for the final chamber.
