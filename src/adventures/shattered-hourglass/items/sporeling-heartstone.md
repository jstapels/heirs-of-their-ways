# Sporeling Heartstone
*Wondrous item, uncommon*

A dense, pulsing fungal node infused with temporal residue.

**Value:** 200 gp to an alchemist or herbalist.

**Crafting:** Can be used to brew a *Potion of Longevity* that reverses aging by 1d6+6 years with no chance of negative effect when brewed this way.
