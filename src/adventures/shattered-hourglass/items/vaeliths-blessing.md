# Vaelith's Blessing
*Wondrous item, uncommon (one-time use)*

A lingering chord of Vaelith's song, humming with possibility.

**Blessing:** Once before your next long rest, when you cast a spell that requires a saving throw, you can force one target to make that save with disadvantage.
