# Torvath's Blessing
*Wondrous item, uncommon (one-time use)*

A warm ember of the ancestor Torvath's resolve.

**Blessing:** Once before your next long rest, when an ally within 30 feet would be reduced to 0 hit points, you can use your reaction to reduce them to 1 hit point instead.
