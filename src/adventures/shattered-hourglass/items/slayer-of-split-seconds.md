# Slayer of Split Seconds
*Weapon (longsword), rare (requires attunement)*

A narrow blade etched with marks that only appear while in motion.

**Properties:**
- +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls.
- Once per turn, when you hit, deal an extra [[/damage 1d6 force]].
- Once per long rest, you can take the Dash action as a bonus action.

**Ending Link:** Recommended reward for the **Slay** ending.
