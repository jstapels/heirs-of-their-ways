# Trident of Fish Command
*Weapon (trident), rare (requires attunement)*

A coral-and-steel trident decorated with fish motifs that always feels slightly wet.

**Properties:**
- +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls.
- While holding the trident, you can cast *Dominate Beast* (save DC 15) on a beast with a natural swim speed. Once used, this property can't be used again until the next dawn.

**Source:** Veth'sora's hoard.
