# Embers of Blood

*Adventure for 4 characters of level 6, designed for 1-2 sessions.*

## Overview

A grim-faced traveler named **Dain Stonefist** arrives at the Bottomless Goat and spends two days becoming a welcome face around the inn. He claims to be a ranger hunting a goblin warband that has been raiding trappers on the edge of Loamvale.

His true motive is revenge.

Dain is Mara Stonefist's older brother. After hearing that Mara was captured in Session 7 and then killed during interrogation in Session 8, Dain has tracked down the adventurers and made a pact with surviving goblins from Session 6. Together they plan a fake "rescue mission" in the woods, then spring an ambush inside a prepared camp.

## Campaign Connections

- **Session 6 Goblin Survivors:** Goblins who escaped regrouped, carrying grudges against the party.
- **Session 8 Consequence:** Mara Stonefist died after Ilizar slit her throat during interrogation, and word of it reached her kin.
- **Loamvale Pressure:** Rumors spread that old enemies now coordinate instead of fighting each other.

## Act 1: The Inn Guest

> *The man at the hearth has shoulders like a timber frame and old burn scars across both hands. He laughs at the right jokes, buys two rounds without fanfare, and never takes the chair with his back to the door.*

Dain spends time with each character, earning trust before asking for help.

### Roleplay Goals

- Let each PC have a one-on-one moment with Dain.
- Dain learns party strengths, preferred marching order, and healing habits.
- Seed small tells: he asks careful questions about Mara but never says her name.

### Social Checks

- [[/check Insight 15]] catches that Dain's grief spikes whenever the caves are mentioned.
- [[/check Perception 14]] notices goblin-fletched arrows hidden in his gear despite his "lone hunter" story.
- [[/check History 12]] or [[/check Survival 12]] identifies his sigil as Stonefist clanwork.

## Act 2: The False Hunt

Dain asks the party to help crush a goblin camp in the Briar Hollow woods.

> *Between black pines, orange firelight flickers over crude tents and sharpened stakes. The goblins look sloppy from a distance: loud, drunk, and vulnerable.*

The camp is staged. Dain has already planned lanes of fire, fallback routes, and signal calls.

### Setup Clues

- [[/check Investigation 14]] finds trip lines hidden under leaf litter.
- [[/check Nature 13]] spots herb bundles tied near the firepit that do not match goblin cooking practice.
- [[/check Insight 16]] notices Dain delaying his first attack until everyone is in the same kill box.

## Final Encounter: Smoke of the Numb Pyre

Just before Dain springs the trap, a goblin alchemist throws a resin sack into the central fire.

### Environmental Twist (Core Mechanic)

**Numbing Smoke (Encounter Rule):**
- A 30-foot-radius cloud spreads from the campfire at initiative count 20 (losing initiative ties).
- Creatures in the smoke must make [[/save CON 14]]. On a failure, they are **Numbed** for 1 minute.
- While Numbed, a creature can't accurately gauge injury: the player does **not** know their current HP total.
- Mechanically, track HP secretly for affected PCs until they leave the smoke for a full round or succeed on a repeat [[/save CON 14]] at end of turn.
- A creature that succeeds on two saves against this effect is immune for 24 hours.

### Encounter Composition (Hard-Deadly)

- @UUID[Compendium.heirs-of-their-ways.heirs-actors.EmBlDainStonef01]{Dain Stonefist} (high-level ranger, primary threat)
- @UUID[Compendium.heirs-of-their-ways.heirs-actors.EmBlSkirkAshgut1]{Skirk Ashgut} (goblin smoke shaman)
- 6-10 Goblins (use MM 2024 Goblin baseline)
- 2 Goblin Bosses (use MM 2024 Goblin Boss baseline)

### Dain's Betrayal Trigger

When at least two PCs are Numbed, Dain uses a prearranged whistle and opens with ranged focus fire on the party's backline.

### Alternate Resolutions

- **Expose Before Springing Trap:** If the party confronts Dain early, some goblins break and flee.
- **Turn the Goblins:** [[/check Intimidation 17]] or [[/check Persuasion 18]] during round 1 can cause half the goblins to disengage if Dain is bloodied.
- **Capture Dain:** If spared, Dain can reveal who funded his travel and who told him where to find the party.

## Rewards

- **Consumable:** 2x Potions of Greater Healing.
- **Consumable:** 1x Dust of Disappearance (in Dain's contingency kit).
- **Permanent:** Emberwood Longbow (+1 longbow; once per long rest, add [[/damage 1d6 fire]] on a hit).
- **Story Reward:** Surviving goblins either become recurring assassins or a coerced information source.

## Aftermath Hooks

- A black-feather courier carries a payment chit addressed to Dain from an unnamed patron.
- If Dain dies, his journal names Mara and confirms this was personal revenge, not random banditry.
- Lucien Darkwood's network may be reactivating old enemies around Loamvale.
