# Coral Trident +1
*Weapon (trident), uncommon*

A trident crafted from magically hardened coral with faint phosphorescence along its shaft.

**Properties:**
- +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls.
- When fully submerged, attacks deal an additional [[/damage 1d6 cold]] damage.
- Two copies are available in the Coral Veil armory.

**Source:** Coral Veil armory.
