# Aboleth's Eye
*Wondrous item, uncommon (requires attunement)*

A fist-sized orb of amber resin containing a preserved aboleth eye that occasionally blinks.

**Properties:**
- You can breathe underwater.
- You have darkvision out to 120 feet while underwater.
- Once per long rest, you can cast *Detect Thoughts* (save DC 13).

**Curse:** You have disadvantage on saving throws against being charmed by aberrations while attuned to the eye.

**Source:** Veth'sora's hoard.
