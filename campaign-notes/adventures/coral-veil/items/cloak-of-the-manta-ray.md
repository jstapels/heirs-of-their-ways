# Cloak of the Manta Ray
*Wondrous item, uncommon*

A deep blue cloak that transforms into manta-like wings when underwater.

**Properties:** While wearing the cloak with its hood up, you can breathe underwater and have a swimming speed of 60 feet. Pulling the hood up or down requires an action.

**Source:** Coral Veil armory.
