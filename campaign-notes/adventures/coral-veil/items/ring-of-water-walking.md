# Ring of Water Walking
*Ring, uncommon*

A simple silver band set with a small blue pearl.

**Properties:** While wearing the ring, you can stand on and move across any liquid surface as if it were solid ground.

**Source:** Carried by Ripple and offered if her father is saved.
