# Thalindra Wavecrest
*Sea elf messenger and desperate quest giver*

A blue-green skinned sea elf whose gills flutter anxiously whenever she is out of the water. Scars from translucent aboleth mucus burns mark her forearms. She speaks halting Common but is determined to save Coral Veil.

## Biography {.system.details.biography.value}
- Granddaughter of Elder Coralline; daughter of Pearlescent.
- Escaped Veth'sora's psychic grip long enough to reach the surface and beg the party for aid.
- Knows the village layout, the timeline of the crisis, and that her mother now serves as the aboleth's voice.
- Carries four **Pearls of Water Breathing** to help the party reach the village.

## Roleplaying Notes
- Exhausted and terrified but still hopeful.
- Can wait in the shallows while the party explores or accompany them briefly with magical water support.
- Pleads for a chance to save her mother if possible.
