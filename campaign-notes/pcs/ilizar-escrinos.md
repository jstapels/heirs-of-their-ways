## Description

A young human who often arrives disheveled with a nonchalant attitude. Quick to hit on attractive women and slow to understand the seriousness of situations.

## Family Connection

Youngest and only male heir of the Escrinos family, making him the **unwilling heir apparent** to the Escrinos Estate. His reluctance to accept his title and responsibility has not gone unnoticed.

### Family Dynamics
- **Father Alejandro** - Head of the Escrinos Estate
- **Sister Valentina** - Eldest, capable and driven, runs much of the business. Most people don't understand why she isn't the heir instead.
- **Sister Isabella** - Second child, constantly compared to their late mother
- **Cousin Mateo** - Head of security, often called "Ilizar's Babysitter" for spending so much time tracking Ilizar down

Many resent Ilizar for neither deserving nor wanting the inheritance. The people of Loamvale view him as an aloof, no-good troublemaker.

## Personality

- Impulsive (blew the Hunter's Whistle immediately just to see what would happen)
- Clever in combat but sometimes makes questionable decisions
- Can be ruthless when frustrated
- Has dark moments and nightmares

## Notable Abilities

- Expert at stealth and infiltration
- Skilled with bow and daggers
- Can cast Mage Hand and other utility magic
- Shield spell for emergency defense
- Uses illusions creatively ("Over there!")
