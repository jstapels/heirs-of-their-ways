# Session 7 - Mara Stonefist

## Overview

The party battles through an ambush behind a waterfall, explores deeper into the caves, and faces off against the bandit leader Mara Stonefist in a brutal confrontation.

## Key Events

### Ambush Behind the Waterfall

**Initial Surprise:**
- Mishko discovers bandits and mephits waiting
- Eyes adjust, hears clicks and twangs of crossbows
- Bandits were expecting goblins, taken aback
- All three shots completely miss Mishko

**The Battle:**
- Mats catches up to Mishko
- Blows magical Hunter whistle to alert others
- Mats and Mishko hold out until reinforcements arrive
- Mephits put several members to sleep (causing scary moments)
- Tides appear to turn against the party
- Group rallies and overcomes enemies

### Cave Exploration

**Bandit Rest Area:**
- Small area where bandits would relax
- Deck of Battlecaster cards spread out in middle of unfinished game
- Party collects the cards
- Mishko discovers a decent-looking lyre in the corner
- Group uses area to catch breath and take short rest

### Confrontation with Mara Stonefist

Deeper in the caves, party finds larger open area:
- Woman with fiery red hair standing atop small rise in rocks
- She identifies herself as Mara Stonefist, leader of the bandits

**Parley:**
- Group explains they've taken her fort
- Mara frustrated: "that fort was supposed to be my backup plan, since things aren't going very well here"
- Unable to get more information
- Combat begins

**The Battle:**
- Tough fight, Mara is savage with her greataxe
- Massive damage when she hits
- Gets extra help from magical ring causing axe to dig deeper
- Mishko desperately quaffs mysterious Fey potion from Cornelius
- Potion causes entire group to disappear, changing battle's edge
- Mats cleverly douses Mara in oil flask, then lights her on fire with torch
- Group uses nearly everything at their disposal
- Exhausted, sore, nearly out of healing potions
- Finally manage to dispatch bandits and knock Mara out
- Battle hard-fought due to bandits' uncanny ability to never miss
- Mats quickly ties her up

## NPCs Encountered

- Mara Stonefist - Fiery red-haired leader of the bandit group (defeated and captured)

## Combat Encounters

**Waterfall Ambush:**
- Multiple bandits with crossbows
- Multiple mephits
- Victory: Party victory after difficult fight

**Mara Stonefist's Chamber:**
- Mara Stonefist (elite warrior with greataxe)
- Additional bandits
- Victory: Hard-fought party victory, Mara captured

## Items Found

- Deck of Battlecaster cards (from unfinished game)
- Decent lyre (claimed by Mishko)
- Magical ring worn by Mara (enhances weapon damage)
- Used: Fey potion from Cornelius (caused party to turn invisible)

## Notable Details

- Mephits putting party members to sleep was a major tactical challenge
- The fort was Mara's "backup plan" suggesting something going wrong with main plan
- Mara's ring is a powerful magical item
- Bandits have unusually high accuracy
- Party is running low on resources after this fight
- Cornelius's Fey potion proved instrumental in turning the tide

## Cliffhanger

Mara Stonefist is captured and unconscious. Questions remain about what she was looking for in the caves and what her primary plan was.
