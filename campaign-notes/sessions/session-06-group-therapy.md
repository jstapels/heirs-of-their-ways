# Session 6 - Group Therapy Session

## Overview

In the aftermath of the keep assault, party members experience various psychological struggles. After recovering, they interrogate their captive, explore the keep, and head north toward the mines, encountering goblins in battle with more bandits.

## Key Events

### Post-Battle Trauma

**Threk's Episode:**
- Feels confined by the keep after being enlarged
- Spaces too small, not enough room even in the middle
- Runs outside to the open northern field
- Mishko follows from a distance
- Sits down, tries to control breathing and empty mind
- Imaginary walls continue pressing down
- Mishko drops enlargement spell, attempts to help with song and dance
- Sings upbeat, lively song of conquering foes
- Takes time, but Threk eventually calms down

**Alara's Battle Craze:**
- Still in heat of battle, running parapets looking for enemies
- Ilizar attempts to calm her, fears she might kill captive
- Repeatedly reminds her of "The Mission"
- Eventually snaps out of it, embarrassed
- Heads to quiet corner to calm down

**Mats' Struggle:**
- Feels haunted by his past
- Able to focus on current situation and overcome feelings of regret and fear

### Interrogating Greta

Party explores keep and interrogates their captive:

**Information Gained:**
- "Mara" gave orders to come down and cause trouble
- Mara is in charge of the bandit group, currently up north in the mines
- Hired to look for something in the mines (Greta doesn't know what)
- Group knocks her back out and prepares her for travel

**Alara's Decision:**
- Feels sheepish about her behavior
- Will remain behind to bundle supplies and get them to Rivermeet
- Party prepares to head north

### Cornelius Returns

On the path north, the party encounters Cornelius again:
- Thanks them for helping him
- Offers each PC a strange Fey potion as gratitude

### Battle with Bandits

Group picks up trail continuing north:
- Eventually hear water and sounds of battle
- Goblins attacking another group of bandits near a waterfall
- Party joins battle, helping goblins
- Bandits quickly overwhelmed by numbers

**Aftermath:**
- Mishko engages goblin leaders in conversation
- Attempts to convince them to join forces and take back the cave
- Party hands over prisoner as gesture of good will
- Goblins explain they won't show others where their "secret" cave is
- Prefer to wait for humans to leave rather than risk losing battle inside
- Goblins respect the group and will leave them alone
- Goblins retreat

### Discovery Behind the Waterfall

- Party searches for cave entrance on their own
- Mishko looks behind the waterfall
- Discovers another group of bandits and mephits waiting in ambush

## NPCs Encountered

- Greta - Captured bandit (interrogated, handed to goblins)
- Cornelius - Pombero Fey, returns to thank the party
- Goblin leaders - Respect party but won't ally with them
- Alara 'Swiftwind' - Stays behind to manage supplies

**Mentioned:**
- Mara - Leader of bandits, currently in the mines

## Combat Encounters

**Bandit vs. Goblin Battle:**
- Party assists goblins
- Bandits quickly defeated
- Victory: Allied with goblins, bandits defeated

## Items Found

- Stolen supplies and mining equipment in keep
- Strange Fey potions from Cornelius (one per party member)

## Notable Details

- Threk struggles with claustrophobia/confined spaces
- Alara has battle rage issues
- Mats is haunted by his past but manages to keep control
- The bandits were looking for something specific in the mines
- Goblins are patient and strategic, not willing to risk their cave
- Mephits waiting behind waterfall suggest elemental involvement

## Cliffhanger

Mishko discovers bandits and mephits lying in ambush behind the waterfall.
