# Session 8 - Don't Meph' with Me

## Overview

The party interrogates Mara Stonefist, ultimately killing her when her cooperation fails. They explore deeper into the cave system, discovering a mysterious elemental fountain and encountering more mephits before hearing voices from below.

## Key Events

### Interrogating Mara

Mara isn't forthcoming with details:

**Information Extracted:**
- Was hired for a job
- Stealing shipments at the fort was a backup plan
- When asked about Terrak, she clams up
- Mats threatens to kill her if she doesn't talk
- Agrees to tell them anything if they promise to let her go
- Mats agrees to the deal

**Details Learned:**
- Mara and crew were hired to mine (didn't know what they were looking for)
- Terrak and his associate constantly oversaw the operation
- Mara didn't know the associate, always dealt with Terrak
- Managed to catch that the associate's name was Selene
- When asked when Terrak and Selene would return: "she never saw them leave"

**Threk's Discovery:**
- Finds a hole with ladder underneath a rug
- Ladder goes much farther down

**Mara's Death:**
- Group asks about the hole
- Mara responds sarcastically: "care to explain what a hole in the ground is?"
- Group punches Mara in face
- She taunts: "what's the matter, you don't like my answer?"
- Ilizar, fed up with snarky answers, slits her throat

### Investigation and Rest

- Group investigates area and bodies
- Takes a long rest
- Finds items on Mara and bandits

### Deeper into the Caves

Party descends ladder and explores:

**Mephit Battle:**
- Comes across group of mud and dust mephits
- Methodically destroys them

**The Elemental Fountain:**
- Strange area with old fountain
- Four stone slabs around fountain representing four base elements:
  - Fire
  - Earth
  - Air
  - Water
- Party experiments with adding elements to basin
- Must have picked wrong order
- Elements retaliate, causing damage
- Decide to investigate later

**Descending Further:**
- Continues and finds stairs going deeper
- Heads downward
- Hears voices of gruff male and lithe female
- Possibly Terrak and Selene?
