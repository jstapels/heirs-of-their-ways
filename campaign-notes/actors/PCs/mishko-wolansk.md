## Description

Lanky in nature with human-esque features. What is most obvious about Mishko (/Meech-coh Voh-lansk/) is the scales covering parts of his body. He has green hair and typically wears a three-piece suit (sans jacket) in earthy brown tones.

## Background - Musician

### Personality Traits
- Believes anything worth doing is worth doing right - a perfectionist
- Strives to do his best with each iteration of artisan work
- Always wants to know how things work and what makes people tick

### Ideal
**Community.** It is the duty of all civilized people to strengthen the bonds of community and the security of civilization. (Lawful) Believes all actions should amount to a neutral net judgment. It is naive to think the world would survive if only "good" was able to thrive.

### Bond
The tools he uses for his trade are the most important items in the world to him.

### Flaw
- Distracted by new gadgets
- Has an affinity for brewed ales and similar beverages
- Driven to don the most ornate robes in history

## Family Connection

Son of **Josep Wolansk**, the patriarch and master brewer of the Wolansk Estate. Josep hoped this adventure would make Mishko "a man... a *hard* man," since Mishko isn't interested in brewing. Mishko has an older sister **Dariya** who guards the Willowrush River.

## Notable Abilities

- Expert dancer and performer
- Skilled at diplomacy and negotiation
- Uses music to inspire allies and confuse enemies
- Mirror Image spell has saved him multiple times
- Can communicate with aquatic creatures

## Campaign Highlights

- Calmed the ghost Aveline Rosewood with a somber love-tune
- Attempted to negotiate with goblins and bandits alike
- Convinced his companions to help with various sidequests
- Performed "The Ballad of Threk's Love for Aveline"
- Showed remarkable resilience despite terrible dice luck
