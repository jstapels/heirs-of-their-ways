# Session 9 - Rockin' Out with Terrak

## Overview

The party confronts Terrak and briefly encounters Selene in a chamber with a floating orb. After a failed parley, they battle Terrak, who transforms into an earth elemental before being defeated. The caves collapse, and the party discovers a hidden Fey sanctuary.

## Key Events

### Meeting Terrak and Selene

Party enters chamber illuminated by soft glow of floating orb:

**Terrak:**
- Hulking figure with stern, unyielding presence
- Arms crossed, revealing jagged tattoo on left forearm
- Tattoo: spiral of cracked stone encircling smaller glowing rune
- Speaks in gravelly voice

**Selene:**
- Lithe and slender figure, keeps watchful distance
- Tattoo on wrist: crescent moon entwined with silver vines
- Nervous, urges Terrak they should leave
- Takes the floating orb and quickly leaves through side passage

**Initial Exchange:**
- Terrak notes they've taken care of Mara
- Says she was "too independent" and needed to learn her place
- Selene warns they've seen too much, should be gone already

### Failed Parley

- Party tries to glean information from Terrak
- Terrak attempts to assess what they know
- Adventurers repeatedly ask about "L.D."
- Terrak loses patience, offers to "give" them something for "L.D."
- Thinly veiled excuse to punch Threk in face
- Party sees through ruse, prepares for combat

### Battle with Terrak - First Form

**Initial Fight:**
- Battle starts one-sided, Terrak struggling to land hits
- During fight, he lets slip the name "Lucien"
- After hard-fought battle, party defeats Terrak

**Transformation:**
- Cavern shakes violently, all lights go out
- Terrak collapses into ground
- Rises again as hulking elemental creature of earth and stone
- Additional dust and mud mephits join the fray

### Battle with Elemental Terrak

**Combat Highlights:**
- Fight intensifies with elemental form
- Mephits remain largely ineffective
- Cavern grows increasingly unstable
- Terrak hurls boulder, knocking Threk down
- Mats keeps distance, hurling elemental attacks
- Takes out mephits and wounds Terrak
- Threk retaliates with repeated sword strikes
- Ilizar less effective with bow but chips away steadily
- Mishko stays on periphery, concentrating on Enlarge spell
- Heals injured allies, fends off irritating mud mephit

**Victory:**
- After grueling battle, adventurers defeat Terrak
- He collapses into rubble

### Cave-In and Discovery

**Collapse:**
- Cavern begins to cave in
- Adventurers knocked out temporarily
- Ilizar wakes first, rushes to aid bleeding Threk

**Trapped:**
- Passage Selene escaped through has caved in
- Tunnel to ladder also collapsed
- Party is trapped

**Hidden Exit:**
- Return to strange Fey fountain
- Find it now activated
- Manage to trigger fountain's mechanism
- Reveals a hidden door
- Follow secret passage descending further
- Arrive at vast chamber with water and stone bridge
